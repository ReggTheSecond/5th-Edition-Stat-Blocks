The Abyss Watcher

Medium undead, unaligned

Armor Class

18 (Scale armor)

Hit Points

155 ()

Speed

40 ft.

Challenge:

11 (7200 XP)

Damage Immunities

poison, psychic

Condition Immunities

poisoned

Senses

dark vision 60ft., passive Perception 13

Languages

Understands Common, cannot speak any language

Broken Mind, Broken Body.

While the Abyss Watcher is not bloodied, at the beginning of each of their turns a Shadow of the Abyss Watcher rises. If two more more Shadows of the Abyss Watcher are active, and if one or all are closer to the Abyss Watcher than any other foe, the Abyss Watcher will attack the Shadows of the Abyss Watcher.

Bloodied Condition.

When the Abyss Watcher becomes bloodied for the first time in a day, each Shadow of the Abyss Watch collapses and decintergrate. And the Abyss Watcher returns to maximum Hit Points.

Cinders of the Abyss.

When the Abyss Watcher becomes bloodied for the first time in a day, any creature that comes within 10ft. of the watcher takes 1d6+1 fire damage, the same is true for any creature that starts its turn within 10ft. of the watcher. And all melee attacks the watcher makes now count as magical damage and deal an additional 1d6+1 fire damage.

Overgrown Humaniod.

The Abyss Watcher is much taller than the avgerage humaniod, standing over seven feet tall with lanky arms and legs. The Abyss Watch counts as one size larger than their size marked above. The Abyss Watcher can use their Greatsword as though it were a versatile weapon.

Actions

Multiattack.

The Abyss Watcher makes two melee attacks, one with their Greatsword and one with their parrying sword.

Greatsword.

Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d12 + 8) slashing damage, if the Abyss Watcher is bloodied they deals an additional (1d6 + 1) fire damage.

Parrying Sword.

Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 8) piercing damage, if the Abyss Watcher is bloodied they deals an additional (1d6 + 1) fire damage.

Leap Attack.

Instead of using its movement and attack actions the Abyss Watcher may leap forward up to 60 ft. the leap must end beside an enemy; this does not provoke an attack of opportunity. After landing the Abyss Watcher attacks with their Greatsword.

Reactions

Parry.

The Abyss Watcher adds 2 to its AC against one melee Attack that would hit it. To do so, the Abyss Watcher must see the attacker and be wielding a melee weapon. The Abyss Watch may make a melee weapon attack against the attacker.
