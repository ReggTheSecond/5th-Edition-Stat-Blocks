13

Senses:

Languages:

any languages it knew in life

Aura of Sobs.

All of the voices that an attic whisperer steals linger around it in an invisible but audible aura of childlike whimpers, songs, and sobs. Any living creature that enters this area loses the benefit of all bardic inspiration and takes a -1 penalty on all attack rolls, damage rolls and wisdom saving throws. The Attic Whisperer can suppress or reactivate its aura as a bonus action. This effects anyone within 30 ft. that is not deaf.

Actions

Bite.

Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.

Hit (1d4 +3) piercing damage

Steal Breath.

When the Attic Whisperer makes a bite attack against a living creature that creature makes a DC13 Charisma saving throw. On a failed save the creature falls asleep until an hour passes or until another creature spends an action to wake them, or until they take damage.

Steal Voice.

The Attic Whisperer touches a living creature, that creature makes a DC13 Charisma saving throw. On a failed save the creature loses it ability to speak for an hour. For that time the Attic Whisperer can speak in that creatures voice, an ally that hears the vioce from the Attic Whisperer may make a DC13 Charisma.
