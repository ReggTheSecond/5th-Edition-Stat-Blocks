telepathy 20ft.

Aura of Attraction.

Each creature within 60ft. of the Banshrae Musicians music makes a DC 14 charisma save, on a failed save they must move at least 10 ft. closer to the Banshrae Musician and cannot make an attack against the Banshrae Musicians. A creature under this effect may attempt to make the save again at the end of each it their turns.

Aura of Drowsiness.

Each creature within 30ft. of the Banshraes music makes a DC 14 charisma save or become slowed. At the beginning of their next turn they make the save again, if they save they awaken, otherwise they fall asleep until they take damage or until another creature takes an action to wake them up.

Aura of Death.

Each creature within 30ft. of the Banshraes music makes a DC 14 wisdom save on a failed save they become frightened for up to conminute. Each creature remakes the wisdom save, on a failed save they take 4d10 psychic damage. On a successful save the spell ends and they can't become effected by the Aura of Death

Actions

Bite.

Melee Weapon Attack: + to hit, reach ft., one target.
