Bone Spikes.

If a creature starts its turn within 5 ft. of the Bone Golem it take 1d6 piercing damage.

Osseous Retaliation.

A creature that makes an opportunity attack against a bone golem takes 2d6 piercing damage.

Magic Weapon.

The golem's weapon attacks are magical.

The golem has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

The golem is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.
