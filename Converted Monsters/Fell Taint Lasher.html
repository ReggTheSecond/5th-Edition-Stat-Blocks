Fell Taint Feeding.

Targets a helpless or unconscious creature; the fell taint lasher loses insubstantial and its fly speed until the end of its next turn, and it makes a coup de grace against the target. If the lasher kills the target, it regains all of its hit points.

Flowing Tendrils.

When the Fell Taint Lasher makes an attack of opportunity it may move upto 5 ft.

Actions

Tendril Caress.

The Fell Taint Lasher reaches out its tendrils towards a single target within 5 ft., the target makes a DC 13 dexterity save, on a failed save the target takes 1d6 + 3 psychic damage.

Tendrils of Stasis.

The Fell Taint Lasher reaches out its tendrils towards a single target within 5 ft., the target makes a DC 13 wisdom save, on a failed save the target takes 1d4 + 3 psychic damage and the target is Restrained, save ends.
