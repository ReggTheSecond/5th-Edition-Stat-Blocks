Fell Taint Feeding.

Targets a helpless or unconscious creature; the fell taint lasher loses insubstantial and its fly speed until the end of its next turn, and it makes a coup de grace against the target. If the lasher kills the target, it regains all of its hit points.

Actions

Tendril Caress.

The Fell Taint Lasher reaches out its tendrils towards a single target within 5 ft., the target makes a DC 14 dexterity save, on a failed save the target takes 1d6 + 3 psychic damage.

Thought Fog.

Each enemy creature in a 25 ft. cone makes a DC 13 intelligence save, each creature who fails the save has its speed requred to half. Creatures who fail to save on subsequent turns are paralyzed until they save.
