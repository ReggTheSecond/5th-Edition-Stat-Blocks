Keen Hearing and Smell

Advantage on wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell.

Regeneration.

The Maelephant regenerates 1d6 + 3 hit points at the start of each or its turns.

Innate Spellcasting.

The Maelephant's spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 18). The Maelephant can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

1/round total: alarm, ensnaring strike, light

3/day total: entangle, gust of wind, magic weapon, polymorph

1/day each: true seeing

Actions

Multiattack.

The Maelephant make three attacks, two with its claws and one with its trunk.

Claw.

Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5ft., one target.

Hit 2d10 +4 slashing damage

Trunk.

Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5ft. to 30ft., one target.

Hit 2d8 +4 bludgeoning damage

Bull Rush.

The Maelephant changes at least 15ft. and a maximum of 45 ft. in a stright line and must be able to end the charge with an attack.

Maelephant Breath Weapon (Recharges 5-6).

The Maelephant breaths a nocious gas that encompases a 30 ft. cone. Any creature cought in this cone must make a DC16 constutation saving throw, on a failed save the creature loses all memory pernamently or until a restoration spell is cast on them

Grasp with Trunk.

When the Maelephant hits with a Trunk attack it may choose to forgo dealing damage it may make make a grapple check against the target of the attack.
