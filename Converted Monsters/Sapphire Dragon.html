Legencary Resistance (3/Day).

If the dragon fails a a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

Innate Spellcasting.

The Sapphire Dragon spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 22, +14 to hit). The Sapphire Dragon can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

1/day each: antimagic field

3/day each: light, stone shape

6/day each: passwall

Actions

Multiattack.

The dragon can use its Frightful Presence. It then makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws.

Bite.

Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.

Hit 10 (2d10 + 5) piercing damage

Claw.

Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.

Hit 10 (2d6 + 5) shashing damage

Tail.

Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.

Hit 10 (2d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage

Frightful Presence.

Each creature of the dragon's choice that is within 120 ft. of the dragon and aware of it must succeed on a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw or become Frightened for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a creature's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the creature is immune to the dragon's Frightful Presence for the next 24 hours.

Sonic Breath (Recharge 5-6).

The Dragon exhales a high-pitched, almost inaudible sound in a 60-foot cone. Each creature in that line must make DC 21 Dexterity saving throw, taking 12d8 psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. Each creature who fails to save must makes a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw, becoming frightened, for 1d8 rounds on a failed save or half as many on a successful one.
