Young Iron Dragon

Medium Dragon, Neutral

Armor Class

15 (Natural Armor)

Hit Points

117

Speed

30 ft., fly 55 ft., swim 15 ft.

Damage Immunities

None

Condition Immunities

None

Damage Resistances

Lightning

Senses

Passive Perception 13; Darkvision

Saving Throws

Dexterity, Constitution

Languages

Common, Draconic

Challenge

5 ( XP)


Actions

Multiattack.

The dragon can use its Frightful Presence. It then makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws.

Bite.

Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.

Hit 10 (2d6 + 2) piercing damage

Claw.

Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.

Hit 10 (2d8 + 2) piercing damage

Tail.

Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.

Hit 10 (3d4 + 2) piercing damage

Breath Weapons (Recharge 6).

The dragon uses one of the following breath Weapons.

Lightning Breath.

The dragon spits lightning at a target within 50 feet. The targeted creature makes a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 5D6 damage lightning damage on a failed save, and the target is restrained. The target takes half as much on a successful one and is not Restrained.
