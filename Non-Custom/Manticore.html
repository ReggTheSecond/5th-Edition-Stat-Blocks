Manticore

large monstrosity, lawful evil

Armor Class

14 (natural armor)

Hit Points

68

Speed

30ft., fly 50 ft.

Senses

darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13

Languages

Common

Challenge:

(3 700 XP)

Tail Spike Regrowth.

The manticore has twenty-four tail spikes. Used spikes regrow when the manticore finishes a long rest.

Actions

Multiattack.

The manticore makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws or three with its tail spikes.

Bite.

Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.

Hit: 5 (d +3) piercing damage.

Claw.

Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.

Hit: 5 (d +3) slashing damage.

Tail Spikes.

Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 100/200ft., one target.

Hit: 5 (d +3) piercing damage.
