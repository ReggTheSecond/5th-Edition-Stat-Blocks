Lady Bela Warren

Medium humaniod (Human), Evil

Armor Class

18 (plate)

Hit Points

72

Speed

30

Saving Throws:

Con +4, Wis +2

Senses:

passive Perception 10

Languages:

Common

Brave.

Lady Bela Warren has advantage on saving throws against being frightened.

Actions

Multiattack.

Lady Bela Warren makes two attaks melee attacks with her greatsword.

Greatsword.

Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target.

Hit: (2d6 + 3) slashing damage.

Leadership (Recharges after a short or long rest).

For 1 minute, Lady Bela Warren can utter a special command or warning whenever a nonhostile creature that she can see within 30 feet of her makes an attack roll or a saving throw. That creature can add a d4 to its roll provided it can hear and understand Lady Bela Warren. A creature can benifit from only one leadership die at a time. The effect ends if Lady Bela Warren is incapacitated.

Reactions

Parry.

Lady Bela Warren adds 2 to her AC against one melee attack that would hit it. To do so, Lady Bela Warren must see the attacker and be weilding a melee weapon.
