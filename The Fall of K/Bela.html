Cunning Action.

On each of its turns, Bela can use a bonus action to take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action.

Evasion.

When Bela is subjected to an effect that allows you to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, you instead take no damage if you succeed on the saving throw, and only half damage if you fail.

Sneak Attack.

Bela deals an additional 14 (4d6) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of hers that isn;t incapatitated and Bela doesn't have disadvantage on the roll.
