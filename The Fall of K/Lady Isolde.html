Lady Isolde

Medium undead, chaotic evil

Armor Class

20 (plate, shield)

Hit Points

180

Speed

30 ft.

Saving Throws

Dex +6, Wis +9, Cha +11

Damage Immunities

necrotic, poison

Condition Immunities

exhaustion, frightend, poisoned

Senses

darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 3

Languages

Abyssal, Common

Challenge:

17 (18000 XP)

Magic Resistance.

Lady Isolde has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

Marshal Undead.

Unless Lady Isolde is incapacitated, she and undead creatures of her choice within 60 feet of it have advantage on saving throws against that turn undead.

Spellcasting.

Lady Isolde is a 19th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 19, +11 to hit with spell attacks). She has the following spells prepared:

1st level (4 slots): command, compelled duel, searing smite

2nd level (3 slots): hold person, magic weapon

3rd level (3 slots): dispel magic, elemental weapon

4th level (3 slots): banishment, staggering smite

5th level (2 slots): destructive wave (necrotic)

Actions

Multiattack.

The armor makes two melee attacks.

Longsword.

Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.

Hit: 1d8 +5 slashing damage, or 1d10 +5 slashing damage if used with two hands, plus 4d8 necrotic.

Hellfire Orb 1/Day.

Lady Isolde hurls a magical ball of fire that explodes at a point of she can see within 120 ft. if her. Each creature in a 20 foot radius sphere centred on that point must make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw. The sphere spreads around corners. A creature takes 10d6 fire damage and 10d6 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Reactions

Parry.

Lady Isolde adds 6 to her AC against one melee attack that would hit her. To do so, Lady Isolde must see the attacker and be weilding a melee weapon.
